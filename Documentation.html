Introduction

DISAL Tunnel is a secure, high-performance tunneling service designed for university students to expose their local development environments to the public internet instantly.

psychology The Problem it Solves

Students often lack public IP addresses or the ability to configure port forwarding on campus networks. DISAL provides an authenticated bridge between our global edge nodes and your machine.

Quickstart

The fastest way to get your app online is using the standard SSH reverse tunnel command.

1. Provision Credentials

Log in to your DISAL dashboard and generate a new tunnel. You'll receive a unique username and a remote port.

2. Run the Command

$ ssh -R 80:localhost:3000 student@disal.ac.gh

info Replace 3000 with your local application's port.
